Welcome to GESAR!

 

GESAR group (Group for Studies and Environmental Simulations in Water Reservoirs) was criated in 2005 following a R&D project ordered by the brazilian power utility company FURNAS CENTRAIS ELÉTRICAS S.A. to UERJ - State University of Rio de Janeiro, to develop a simulator of the dynamics of hydro power plants reservoirs. Group operations comprise 26 projects, most of them concluded (1 with FURNAS CENTRAIS ELÉTRICAS S.A. and 7 with federal brazilian agencies CNPq, CAPES and FINEP and 18 with State of Rio de Janeiro research financing agency FAPERJ. The projects adressed applied research in numerical simulations, water quality and data basis development, in total value of approximately US$ 3.5 millions. The group is a partner of CWR - Center for Water Research (Australia), UNA - Universidad Nacional de Asuncion (Paraguay), TU Delft (Holland) and with ULB - Free University of Brussels. GESAR is currently an UDT - Technological Development Unit within UERJ - State University of Rio de Janeiro, what facilitates the group to offer applied research services to public and private companies. The UERJ graduate program in Mechanical Engineering originated in the group in 2005 and has awarded 2 PhD and 12 MSc titles. [more info]
